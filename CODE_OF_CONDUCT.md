# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

MechanicsDSL is committed to providing a welcoming environment for everyone contributing to or using the project, regardless of background, experience level, or discipline.

## Our Standards

**Expected behaviour:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome contributions from researchers, engineers, educators, and students at all levels
- Give and receive feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the scientific community

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Dismissing contributions based on the contributor's background or credentials
- Deliberate misinformation about the software's capabilities or limitations

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all MechanicsDSL repositories, issue trackers, pull requests, and any associated communication channels.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to **nomapa223@gmail.com**. All reports will be handled confidentially. Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, close issues, or restrict access for conduct that violates these standards.
